The questions

I didn’t just write some random 150 questions from my head. That would be useless, and I see that sometimes on the internet. Instead, I did a solid research and included the questions from the following sources.

Questions that the interviewers google

First of all, you may not believe it, but 8 out of 10 interviewers I asked confessed that they google the phrase “frontend interview questions” or “js interview questions” and later ask the questions during the interview. Damn, I even did it myself when I was interviewing people in our company!

Questions that the interviewers kept asking me

I went to about 50 interviews myself to see if there’s a pattern to which questions the interviewers in various companies like to ask. At some point, the interviews became rather boring because they all repeated themselves, I got a lot of questions that were EXACTLY the same.

Questions constantly reported to me by other developers

I have a huge network of friends who got hired in great companies, and they regularly deliver me new questions. A lot of the stuff that the interviewer want you to know is the same from company to company.
